Getting Started

  • Saving a guide sends it straight to our official Github repository where editors around the world can help edit.
  • Your guide will show up in the master branch of our Github repository unless you're editing a guide started by someone else. In that case, a branch named after your username will automatically be created. The original author can then see your suggested changes directly on Github.
  • Once our editors decide your article is ready, we'll put your guide on our homepage so your expertise can help educate the world.

Writing

This editor supports markdown, which means you can create great looking guides with special syntax. The editor is designed to work closely with Github.com's markdown implementation. Click the Live Tutorial option above or refer to these Github.com references for help:

  • Basic formatting
  • Mastering markdown

Table of Contents

We'll automatically a create Table of Contents for your guide based on your usage of <h1> and <h2> tags the equivalent '#' and '##' markdown headers.
